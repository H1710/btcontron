What is Bitcoin
on TRON?

Bitcoin on TRON is a community-driven token launched on the TRON network with a vision to create a robust and engaged community.

Our journey begins on the SunPump Meme platform, where we aim to grow a strong social media presence and connect with like-minded individuals.

  • As we expand, we will update our token information across major platforms like DEXTools to ensure transparency and accessibility.
  • Once we reach 5,500 holders, a snapshot will be taken, and members will be rewarded with either a token airdrop or a unique 1-of-1 NFT, highlighting our commitment to giving back to our community.
  • Our long-term goal is to establish strategic partnership programs, supporting and collaborating with other projects within the TRON ecosystem as we continue to grow and evolve.

How to buy

$BTRX

Create Your Safepal Wallet

Download Safepal or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to safepal.com

Get Some TRX

Have TRX in your wallet to swap for $BTRX. If you don’t have any TRX, you can buy directly on Safepal, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Safepal

Connect to Sun.io or use the browser inside your Safepal app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $BTRX token address "Contract", select $BTRX, and confirm. When Safepal prompts you for a wallet signature sign.
